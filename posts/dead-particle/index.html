The Death of a Particle #

Overview #

Imagine you are implementing a particle system for your game. For performance reasons, your ParticleManager class stores and operates on one contiguous (and homogenous) Particles array, containing 1 or more live particles.

These particles are not stored in any particular order and can each be updated and rendered independantly.

During the ParticleManager update loop, one of the particle's lifetimes has expired, which results in this particle being flagged as DEAD, as shown in Figure 1.

Question #

Describe an efficient method for removing the DEAD particle, whilst preserving the contiguity of the array.
