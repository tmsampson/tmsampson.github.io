Interview Question - The Dead Particle

Overview #

Imagine you are implementing a particle system for your game. For performance reasons you would like your update and render loops to operate on one contiguous array of live particles. The particles updates are completely independent so update order is not a problem.

During the previous frame update, you marked a particle in the middle of your array as dead.

Example #

Particles: P0, P1, P2 , ... , P150000 , ... , P999999, P1000000
                         ↑                              
                     ☠ DEAD ☠

Question #

Describe an efficient method for removing the dead particle whilst preserving the contiguous array for the next update.
